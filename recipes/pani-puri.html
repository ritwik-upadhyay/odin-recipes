Pani Puri Recipe

Description

Do you call it Gol Gappa or Pani Puri? Just hearing the name makes your mouth water. It is also called Gol Gappa. If you ever go to Chandni Chowk or Sitaram Bazaar, do not miss the opportunity to eat Golgappe.

Of all, you will find to eat round guppies in restaurants and malls, but you can enjoy round guppies while standing in the market with a cart and watching the market. Water from whole wheat flour, or flour and semolina equally, or from semolina and flour. You can also make round guppies of flour or semolina according to your desire. Here we are making round gape by mixing flour semolina.

Ingredients

  • Wheat flour or flour - half a cup
  • Semolina - 1 cup
  • Oil
  • Green coriander - half a cup of leaves
  • Podina - half a cup of leaves
  • Tamarind or amaranth powder - 2 teaspoons (or 2 lemon juice)
  • Green chili -2
  • Roasted cumin -1 - 2 small spoons
  • Black pepper - 1/4 small spoon
  • Salt - according to taste (one small spoon)
  • Black salt - half a teaspoon

Steps

  1. Take out the flour and semolina in a pot and mix well. Knead the whole dough with the help of lukewarm water (knead the dough well)
  2. Cover the kneaded dough and keep it for 20 minutes to set. The water is made in 2 ways, make a round gap in the way you like.
  3. Cover the kneaded dough and keep it for 20 minutes to set. The water is made in 2 ways, make a round gap in the way you like.
  4. Make small lumps from the kneaded dough. Cover these loaves with a cloth. One by one, take a round ball with a diameter of about 2 inches. Cover these belly puris with a cloth and fry them.
  5. Wash coriander and podine leaves with clean water. Grind all the spices and coriander, podina finely with a mixer. Dissolve the ground spice in 1 liter of water. Take the water made by your hands, cumin water is ready for full.
