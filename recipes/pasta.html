Pasta Recipe

Description

I created this fettuccine Alfredo by modifying my mother's recipe, and it gets nothing but rave reviews every time I make it. My boyfriend is a fettuccine Alfredo connoisseur, and he scrapes the pan clean every time — just be aware that this pasta recipe is not for the health-conscious.

This is one of my all time favourite things to make. You can make this and eat this and you'll always feel content and so relaxed. This is such a wholesome dish. And so so easy to make. Heavenly!

Ingredients

  • 24 ounces dry fettuccine pasta
  • 1 cup butter
  • ¾ pint heavy cream
  • 1 pinch garlic salt
  • salt and ground black pepper to taste
  • ¾ cup grated Romano cheese
  • ½ cup grated Parmesan cheese

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients.
  2. Fill a large pot with lightly salted water and bring to a rolling boil. Cook fettuccine at a boil until tender yet firm to the bite, about 8 minutes. Drain.
  3. Heat butter and cream in a large saucepan over low heat until butter melted; add garlic salt, salt, and black pepper.
  4. Increase the heat to medium; stir in Romano and Parmesan cheeses until melted and sauce has thickened.
  5. Add cooked pasta to sauce; toss until thoroughly coated. Serve immediately.
